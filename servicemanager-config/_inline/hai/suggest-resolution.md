# Suggest Resolution

HAi Suggest Resolution is an AI-powered productivity tool designed to automatically generate user-friendly resolution notes from your IT or customer support ticket records.

* **Simplifies Technical Language**: It translates complex, jargon-heavy fixes into clear, non-technical steps that end-users can easily understand and follow.
* **One-Click Generation**: Agents can simply paste a block of text into the Incident Resolution box, then click the Suggest Resolution button to instantly generate resolution information.
