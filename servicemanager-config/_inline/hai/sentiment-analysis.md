# Sentiment Analysis

HAi Sentiment Analysis in Hornbill evaluates and scores the emotional tone of customer messages, classifying them as positive, negative, or neutral. It helps service desks quickly identify frustrated users so agents can prioritize and appropriately manage high-emotion interactions.

Key features and functions include:

* **Real-Time Scoring**: Evaluates the contextual attitude behind the words (rather than just keyword scanning) and assigns a score representing the emotional state of the customer.
* **Automatic Updates**: Automatically triggers a sentiment check whenever a customer posts to the portal timeline or sends an email updating their support ticket.
* **Agent Visibility**: Provides agents and managers with a quick visual cue of the customer's mood, allowing them to adjust their communication style and de-escalate urgent issues.
