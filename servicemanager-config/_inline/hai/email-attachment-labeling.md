# Email Attachment Labeling

Hornbill's AI (HAi) suite includes an Email Attachment Labeling feature, which automatically analyzes image attachments, generates short descriptive labels, and lets agents quickly unselect unwanted images before processing the ticket.

* **Automated Descriptions**: The AI generates text descriptions for image attachments, making it easier for agents to identify what a file is at a glance.
* **Filters Unwanted Files**: It can detect and automatically unselect or filter out junk, spam, or unwanted attachments (like signature logos or background images) so they aren't needlessly attached to the ticket.
