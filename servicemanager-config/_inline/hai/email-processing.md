# Email Processing

HAi Email Processing uses artificial intelligence to sanitize and structure incoming email text, ensuring it is converted into a highly readable, clean, and easily applicable format before being added to a support ticket or request.

Key capabilities of the HAi Email feature suite include:

* **Text Formatting & Assist**: Cleans up unstructured or raw email text so agents don't have to. Alongside text clean-up, Hornbill's AI features can correct spelling and grammar, change tone, or summarize the email in seconds.
